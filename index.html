About Me

I am Shuaichen Chang, a 5th-year PhD student in Computer Science Department at The Ohio State University, advised by Prof. Eric Fosler-Lussier. My research focuses on building the connection between structure data (database, map image) and natural language text, including semantic parsing and multimodal question answering.

Before coming to OSU, I obtained my bachelor degree from Computer Science Department at Fudan University, advised by Prof. Xipeng Qiu.

I am looking for full-time opportunities in 2023.
