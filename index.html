About Me

I am Shuaichen Chang, a 6th-year PhD student in Computer Science Department at The Ohio State University, advised by Prof. Eric Fosler-Lussier (opens new window). My research focuses on building robust and generalizable natural language interface to structure data (tables, databases, map images). I work on semantic parsing and multimodal question answering.

Before coming to OSU, I obtained my bachelor degree from Computer Science Department at Fudan University, advised by Prof. Xipeng Qiu (opens new window).

I am looking for full-time opportunities in 2023.
