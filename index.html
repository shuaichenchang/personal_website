About Me

I am Shuaichen Chang, a 4th-year PhD student in Computer Science Department at The Ohio State University, advised by Prof. Eric Fosler-Lussier. My research focuses on building the connection between structure data (database, map) and natural language text. Before coming to OSU, I obtained my bachelor degree from Computer Science Department at Fudan University, advised by Prof. Xipeng Qiu.

I have interned with Dr. Jing Huang and Dr. Xiaodong He at JD.COM AI Research, and Dr. Supeng Ge at Pinterest.
