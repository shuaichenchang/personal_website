Context: A database about high school students containing the information about if two students are friends and if a student likes another student.

Below are 2 good examples:
Question: Show the names of high school students and their corresponding number of friends.
Good Paraphrase 1: List all students and how many friends they have.
Good Paraphrase 2: Show a list of high school students along with their number of freinds.

Question: Return the name of the high school student with the most friends.
Good Paraphrase 1: Which student is the most friendly in high school.
Good Paraphrase 2: Which high school student has the most friends?

Below are a bad example:
Question: Show me all grades that have at least 4 students.
Bad Paraphrase 1: Show me all grades that there are more than 4 students have. (It is not a good paraphrase because at least 4 (including 4) does not mean the same as more than 4 (excluding 4))
