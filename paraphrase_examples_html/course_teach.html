Context: A database containing the information about teachers and the courses they teach.

Below are 2 good examples:
Question: What is the total count of teachers?
Good Paraphrase 1: How many teachers are there?
Good Paraphrase 2: Return the total number of teachers.

Question: List the name of teachers whose hometown is not "Little Lever Urban District" .
Good Paraphrase 1: Please list teachers who were not born in Little Lever Urban District.
Good Paraphrase 2: Which teachers are not from Little Lever Urban District?

Below are a bad example:
Question: List the name of teachers whose hometown is not "Little Lever Urban District" .
Bad Paraphrase 1: Show teachers and their hometown. (It is not a good paraphrase because the original question only wants to know the teachter is not from Little Lever Urban District not all teachers.)
Bad Paraphrase 2: How many teachers are not from Little Lever Urban District. (It is not a good paraphrase because the original question asks for the teacher names not the number of teachers.)
