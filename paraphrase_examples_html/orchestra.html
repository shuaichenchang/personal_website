Context: A database about orchestra, including orchestra information and the conductor information and their performance records.

Below are 2 good examples:
Question: What are the names of conductors, ordered by age?
Good Paraphrase 1: Show me all conductors from the yongest to the oldest.
Good Paraphrase 2: List the conductors by their age.

Question: Return the names of conductors that do not have the nationality "USA".
Good Paraphrase 1: Please list non-American conductors.
Good Paraphrase 2: Show all conductors who do not have US nationality.

Below are a bad example:
Question: Show the names of conductors that have conducted more than one orchestras.
Bad Paraphrase 1: Which conductors have conducted at least one orchestras? (It is not a good paraphrase because more than one (excluding 1) does not mean the same as at least one (including 1))
