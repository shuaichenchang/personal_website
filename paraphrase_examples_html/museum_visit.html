Context: A database containing the information of museums, visitors and visit records.

Below are 2 good examples:
Question: How many museums were opened after 2013 or before 2008?
Good Paraphrase 1: How many museums were opened outside of 2008-2013?
Good Paraphrase 2: List the amount of museums that are open either before 2008 or after 2013.

Question: How many visitors below age 30 are there?
Good Paraphrase 1: How many visitors are yonger than 30 years old?
Good Paraphrase 2: How many people have been to museums who are yonger than 30?

Below are a bad example:
Question: How many museums were opened after 2013 or before 2008?
Bad Paraphrase 1: Which museums were opened between 2008 and 2013? (It is not a good paraphrase because the original question asks after 2013 or before 2008 not between.
Bad Paraphrase 2: What museums were opened after 2013 or before 2008? ? (It is not a good paraphrase because the original question asks for the number of museums not their names.)
