Context: A school system database including course arrangement, students' enrollment and students' transcripts.

Below are 2 good examples:
Question: What are the descriptions for all the math courses?
Good Paraphrase 1: Describe the available math courses.
Question: Show the date and id of the transcript with at least 2 course results.
Good Paraphrase 1: For transcripts containing two of more courses, what are their date and ID?
Good Paraphrase 2: For transcripts containing no less than 2 courses, list their date and ID.

Below are a bad example:
Question: Show the date and id of the transcript with at least 2 course results.
Bad Paraphrase 1: For transcripts containing more than 2 courses, what are their date and ID? (It is not a good paraphrase because the at least 2 (including 2) and more than 2 (excluding 2) don't mean the exact same thing.)
Bad Paraphrase 2:What transcripts have more or equal to 2 results? (It is not a good paraphrase because the original question asks for id and date that is omitted in the paraphrase.)
