Context: A database containing the information about stadiums, singers, and concerts.

Below are 2 good examples:
Question: Show the stadium name and capacity with most number of concerts in year 2014 or after.
Good Paraphrase 1: Which stadium has the most concerts in 2014 or later? List the stadium name and its capacity?
Good Paraphrase 2: Show the stadium name and maximum number of seats with highest number of concerts since 2014.

Question: What are the names of the singers who had at least 5 concerts?
Good Paraphrase 1: Which singers had no less than 4 concerts?
Good Paraphrase 2: Who has held at least five concerts?

Below are a bad example:
Question: Show the stadium name and capacity with most number of concerts in year 2014 or after.
Bad Paraphrase 1: Which stadium has the most concerts in 2014 or later? (It is not a good paraphrase because the show capacity in the original question is omitted)
Bad Paraphrase 2: What is the details of the stadium that has the most concerts in 2014 or later? (It is not a good paraphrase because details does not mean the same as name and capacity)
