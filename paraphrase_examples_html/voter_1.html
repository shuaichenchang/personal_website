Context: A database about vote records, including the vote time, location and contestants information.

Below are 2 good examples:
Question:What are the create dates, states, and phone numbers of the votes that were for the contestant named 'Tabatha Gehling'?
Good Paraphrase 1: For those that voted for Tabatha Gehling, list their create dates, states, and cell numbers.
Good Paraphrase 2: Show me the creation dates, states and phone numbers of votes that went to 'Tabatha Gehling'.

Question: How many contestants did not get voted?
Good Paraphrase 1: List the number of contestants that had 0 votes.
Good Paraphrase 2: List the amount of contestants without any vote.

Below are a bad example:
Question: What are the create dates, states, and phone numbers of the votes that were for the contestant named 'Tabatha Gehling'?
Bad Paraphrase: Show the information of votes that go to 'Tabatha Gehling'? (It is not a good paraphrase because information does not mean the same as create dates, states, and phone numbers )
