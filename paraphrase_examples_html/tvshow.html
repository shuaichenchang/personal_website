Context: A database about TV shows, including the information of each channel and the cartoon and tv series on each channel.

Below are 2 good examples:
Question: How many TV Channel using language English?
Good Paraphrase 1: What's the amount of English TV channels?
Good Paraphrase 2: Show me how many TV Channel in English.

Question: What is the maximum and minimum share for the TV series?
Good Paraphrase 1: What is the highest and lowest share for all TV series?
Good Paraphrase 2: List the share range for the TV series. Show maximun and minimum shares.

Below are a bad example:
Question: What is the maximum and minimum share for the TV series?
Bad Paraphrase 1: The TV series has a maximum and minimum share of what? (It is not a good paraphrase because the two questions are asking for different things)
Bad Paraphrase 2: What is the maximum and minimum share for the TV series? (It is not a good paraphrase because the paraphrase is the same as the original question.)
