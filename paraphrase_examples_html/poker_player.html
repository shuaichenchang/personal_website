Context: A database about poker players, including their basic information and poker game records.

Below are 2 good examples:
Question: Return the nationalities for which there are two or more people.
Good Paraphrase 1: What countries has no less than 2 people.
Good Paraphrase 2: Show the nationalities for which there are more than one people.

Question: Return the average earnings across all poker players.
Good Paraphrase 1: How much does poker players make on average?
Good Paraphrase 2: Show me the average winnings of all poker players.

Below are a bad example:
Question: Return the nationalities for which there are two or more people.
Bad Paraphrase 1: What countries has no less than 2 players. (It is not a good paraphrase because people means all people and players only refer to poker players)
Bad Paraphrase 2: Return the nationalities for which there are more than two people? (It is not a good paraphrase because two or more does not mean the same as more than two)
