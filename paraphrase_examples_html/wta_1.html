Context: A database containing WTA (Women's Tennis Association) players information, their ranking and match records.

Below are 2 good examples:
Question: Find the names of loser and winner who played in the match with greatest number of minutes.
Good Paraphrase 1: Who are the loser and winner played in the match that lasted the longest?
Good Paraphrase 2: I want the names of the loser and winner that played in the longest match.

Question: List the first and last name of all players who are left / L hand in the order of birth date.
Good Paraphrase 1: What are the full names of all left handed players, in order of birth date?
Good Paraphrase 2: Show full name of left handed players ordered by their birth date?

Below are a bad example:
Question: Find the names of loser and winner who played in the match with greatest number of minutes.
Bad Paraphrase 1: Find the information of loser and winner who played in the longest match. (It is not a good paraphrase because the original question asks for names. information in the paraphrase is too vague.)
Bad Paraphrase 2: Who are the loser and winner played in the match that lasted the shortest? (It is not a good paraphrase because the original questions asks about the longest game and the paraphrase is about the shortest game.)
