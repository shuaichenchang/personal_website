Context: A database containing the information of all countries in the world as well as major cities of each country.

Below are 2 good examples:
Question: Which continent has the highest population?
Good Paraphrase 1: Which continent has the most people?
Good Paraphrase 2: Show me that continent that have the most residents.

Question: What is the average life expectancy in African countries that are republics?
Good Paraphrase 1: What is the average of life expectancy in republic african countries.
Good Paraphrase 2: List the average life expectancy in African countries that are considered republics.

Below are a bad example:
Question: What is the average life expectancy in African countries that are republics?
Bad Paraphrase 1: What is the average life expectancy in African countries? (It is not a good paraphrase because the republics in the original question is omitted)
Bad Paraphrase 2: What is life expectancy of each African republic country?
(It is not a good paraphrase because the original question asks for the average life expectancy of all African republic countries, not the life expectancy of each country.)
