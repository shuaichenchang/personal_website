Context: A database about dogs and owners as well as the record of the treatments each dog has had.
Each treatment includes the treatment type, the professional (who did the treatment to the dog), date and cost.

Below are 2 good examples:
Question: Tell me the owner id and last name of the owner who spent the most on treatments of his or her dogs.
Good Paraphrase 1: who spent the most on treatments of their dogs? Show me the id and last name.
Good Paraphrase 2: Show me owner id and last name of owner who spent the most on dog care.

Question: Which professionals have done the most number of treatments?
Good Paraphrase 1: Which professionals are the most experienced?
Good Paraphrase 2: Who has held at least five concerts?

Below are a bad example:
Question: Tell me the owner id and last name of the owner who spent the most on treatments of his or her dogs.
Bad Paraphrase 1: Tell me the information of the owner who spent the most on treatments of his or her dogs.
(It is not a good paraphrase because the owner id and last name in the original question is omitted)
Bad Paraphrase 2: Show me owner id and last name of owner whose dogs have the most times of treatments.
(It is not a good paraphrase because the original question asks about the most spent (money), and the paraphrase asks about the most number of treatments.)
