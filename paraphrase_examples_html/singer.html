Context: A database containing the information of singers and their songs.

Below are a few good examples:
Question: what are the different names of the singers that have sales more than 300000?
Good Paraphrase 1: Show me all singers who have sales greater than 300,000?
Good Paraphrase 2: Which singers' selling are more than 300K?

Question: What singer has the highest sales number?
Good Paraphrase: Who is the best selling singer?

Question: What singer has the most number of songs?
Good Paraphrase: Who is the most productive singer?

Below are a bad example:
Question: what are the different names of the singers that have sales more than 300000?
Bad Paraphrase: Show me all singers who have sales at least 300,000? (It is not a good paraphrase because more than 300000 (excluding 300000) does not mean the same as at least 300000 (including 300000))
